Our Services

DS Edutrak develops software solutions that simplify academic administration and institutional management.

Laboratory Stock Management

Manage laboratory inventories with ease by tracking chemicals, reagents, consumables, instruments, procurement records, stock availability, and expiry dates.

Timetable Management

Automatically generate and manage class timetables while minimizing scheduling conflicts for faculty, classrooms, and departments.

Class Allocation System

Efficiently assign classrooms based on department requirements, student strength, laboratory availability, and infrastructure capacity.

Examination Timetable Management

Create optimized examination schedules while avoiding student and faculty conflicts and efficiently utilizing available examination halls.
